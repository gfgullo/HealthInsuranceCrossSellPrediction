Insurance Cross Selling Prediction

This app estimates the opportunity for insurance cross-selling. It uses information obtained from customers who have purchased an health insurance to predict whether they might be interested in purchasing a vehicle insurance as well.

The following is the information used by the system:

  • Gender:gender of the customer
  • Age:age of the customer
  • Driving License:if the customer have a driving license
  • Region Code:unique code for the region of the customer
  • Previously Insured:if customer already has vehicle insurance
  • Vehicle Age:age of the vehicle
  • Vehicle Damage:if customer got his/her vehicle damaged in the past
  • Annual remium:the amount customer needs to pay as premium in the year
  • Policy Sales Channel:anonymized Code for the channel of outreaching to the customer ie. Different Agents, Over Mail, Over Phone, In Person, etc.
  • Vintage:number of days, customer has been associated with the company
